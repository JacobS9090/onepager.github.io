History

Jeopardy has been around since 64 but was only on air for 11 years. Then it was revived in 78 but only lasted a year. Luckily Sony Pictures adopted it in 84. It has left it's mark on the history of television and the history of Gameshows. Jeopardy is hosted by none other than Alex Trebek who has been the host of Jeopardy since 1984. Jeopardy has changed massively since it was created. At one point Contestants who had a hot streak could only be on for five days as a new contestant would replace the winner, but the winner would be guarenteed a spot on the Tournament of Champions. The five day limit was abolished in 2003 which allowed the winning contestant to continue their hot-streak.
